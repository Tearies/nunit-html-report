Quick Tips for Better Gas Mileage

Are you looking to save money at the pump? Don’t miss these quick and easy tips for better fuel economy for your car or truck from JustAnswer Car Experts.

Are you looking to save money at the pump? Don’t miss these quick and easy tips for better fuel economy for your car or truck from JustAnswer Car Experts:

Go for the shade: Did you know that the hot summer sun can cause gas to evaporate right out of your gas tank? Improve your gas mileage by parking in the shade or facing away from the sun. A windshield shade is also a good way to keep heat out of the inside of your car.

Use the right oil:  Using the manufacturer's recommended grade of motor oil can improve your car's gas mileage by 1- 2%. When shopping for oil, also look for the words "energy conserving" on the label.
